About Saqr

Saqr Hospital is a leading healthcare institution committed to providing quality care to patients in Ras Al Khaimah .
Our state-of-the-art facilities and experienced medical professionals make us a preferred destination for individuals seeking advanced medical treatment.

At Saqr Hospital, we believe in a patient-centric approach that prioritizes individualized care and personalized attention.
Our team of doctors, nurses, and support staff work together to provide comprehensive care that meets the unique needs of each patient.
We offer a wide range of medical services, Our commitment to excellence and patient satisfaction has earned us a reputation as a trusted provider of healthcare services in the region.

Join our team and be a part of a healthcare institution that is making a positive impact on the lives of patients and their families.
We offer a supportive work environment, opportunities for professional growth, and competitive compensation packages.

About Emirates Health Services (EHS)

Emirates Health Services was established with the aim of enhancing the efficiency of the federal health sector in the country,
by providing health care and treatment services, and taking preventive measures and combating epidemics and diseases, as well
as achieving sustainable development of health care.
